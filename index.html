About

Charlotte Rogers is a Master's student studying Design and Technology at Parsons School of Design in NYC with a focus in UI/UX Development and Graphic Design. She has a Bachelor's in Graphic Communication with experience in Computer Science and CAD as well. Most projects she has worked on have a focus of Design Justice and accessibility. She is currently based in Brooklyn, NY.
